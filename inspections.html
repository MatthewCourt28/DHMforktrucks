Thorough Examination

Safety is our top priority, which is why our inspection services are thorough and meticulous.

Thorough examination of industrial lift trucks is required under the following health and safety laws: LOLER 1998, which covers lifting equipment, and PUWER 1998, which deals with all other safety-related items, such as brakes, steering and tyres.

Our engineers are fully qualified to carry out a Thorough Examination of your forklifts ensuring compliance with both LOLER and PUWER regulations.
